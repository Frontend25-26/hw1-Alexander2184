Riliane Lucifen d'Autriche

Now, kneel to me!

Bio

Riliane Lucifen d'Autriche (aka The Daughter of Evil) is the former queen of Kingdom of Lucifenia. Her obsession with the demon of Pride made her one of the main figures in Evillious Chronicles.
